Our Story

Born from a
Question

Valyria Scent was not born in a boardroom. It was born from a question that refused to go away: "Why should luxury always come from somewhere else?"

We looked at the perfume world and saw shelves full of foreign names at impossible prices — and we decided Egypt deserved better. Not an imitation of luxury. Real luxury. Built here. By us.

We spent months obsessing over every detail — the ingredients, the bottle, the crown on the cap. Because we believe that when you carry Valyria Scent, you don't just carry a fragrance. You carry a statement.
